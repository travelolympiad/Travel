Categories

Travel Olympiad is a travel based quiz open for students of classes 1 to 10. Considering the syllabi of the classes, Travel Olympiad has categorised into Hudson, Gama and Columbus. The question papers are set different for each category. It is mandatory to register according to the category. Winners are selected category - wise.

Hudson
(1 - 4)

Students of Class 1-4 are into the Hudson Clan. It is the Sub - Junior level. Students need to answer 30 question in 60 minutes.

Gama
(5 - 7)

Students of Class 5-7 are into the Gama Clan. It is the Junior level.Students need to answer 50 question in 60 minutes.

Columbus
(8 - 10)

Students of Class 8-10 are into the Columbus Clan. It is the senior level.Students need to answer 50 question in 60 minutes.
