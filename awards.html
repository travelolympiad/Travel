                                                               AWARDS

The City and School toppers are the winners of the Travel Olympiad. The winners will be the Ambassadors of Travel Olympiad for the next year. They get to represent the Travel Olympiad in their school and also take part in the events organised by Travel Olympiad. The winners for each category will get awards and certificates as per their ranks. Rest all the participants will be given a certificate of participation. 

Globe Trotter

1st ranker at the city level is the Globe Trotter. Globe Trotter will get a
3 days | 2 Nights package worth of Rs.20,000/- + Winner Badge & Certificate of outstanding performance.

Adventurer

Topper of the school is the Adventurer. Adventurer will get 1 day adventure camp worth of Rs.5000/- + Badge of Excellence + Certificate of Excellence.

Hiker

2nd - 10th ranker from a school is the Hiker. Hiker will get a Badge Distinction and a Certificate of Distinction.
