Travel Olympiad  is an initiative taken up to impart the knowledge about the rich culture and heritage of different places among the young Indians. It strives to prepare the young Indian to aspire beyond his immediate geography, to be confident and feel comfortable anywhere in the world.To create a more aspiring, confident Indian, we need to :

  • Get  children to know more about the world so they are open to travelling and are confident to operate out of their comfort zones.
  • We also need to teach them to experientially grasp the History and Geography of the world as a global skill even if they are to pursue a career beyond humanities.

So we came up with the Travel Olympiad for students from class 1 to 10 in three different categories to help them improve the geography and history, to be prepared to face the challenges out of their comfort zone.
